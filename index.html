Privacy Policy for Translate & Play

Last updated: January 2026

Thank you for using Translate & Play. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Non-personal data such as device type, operating system, app version, and usage statistics.
  • In-app activity (e.g., levels completed, hints used, lives remaining) to improve gameplay experience.
  • Ad and analytics identifiers for measuring app performance and ad relevance.
  • Anonymous user identifiers, referral codes, and game settings stored securely on our backend.

2. How We Use the Information

We use collected data to:

  • Improve the app's functionality and user experience.
  • Analyze gameplay statistics to balance difficulty and engagement.
  • Serve relevant advertisements and measure their effectiveness.
  • Enable features like referral rewards and game progress synchronization.

3. Advertising and Analytics

We use third-party services to display ads and analyze app usage, including Google AdMob and Firebase Analytics. These services may collect non-personal data such as device type, operating system, country, session duration, and in-app behavior to improve performance and relevance of ads and app content.

Google AdMob: AdMob may use device identifiers and cookies to deliver personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings (Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization).

Firebase Analytics: We use Firebase Analytics to better understand how users interact with the app. This tool collects anonymized data and does not identify users personally. Learn more: Google Privacy Policy

4. Backend Services

We use Supabase as our backend service to securely store:

  • Anonymous user identifiers for game progress
  • Referral code information when you invite friends
  • Game settings and preferences

This data is stored securely and is not linked to your personal identity. Learn more: Supabase Privacy Policy

5. In-App Purchases

The app offers optional in-app purchases (IAPs) such as hints, lives, or crystals. All payments are processed securely through Google Play Billing. We do not have access to your payment information (such as credit card or Google account details).

6. Data Retention and Security

We retain non-personal data for as long as necessary to improve app functionality. All data is processed securely, and we do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

7. Compliance with Privacy Laws

GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation): If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You may also request to limit or object to its processing by contacting us at translateplayapp@gmail.com.

CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act): If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal data we collect, to request its deletion, and to opt out of any data sale (we do not sell personal data). You can contact us for these requests via the same email address.

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided personal information, please contact us at translateplayapp@gmail.com and we will promptly delete such data.

9. Account and Data Deletion

To request deletion of your account and associated data:

  1. Open the app and go to Settings
  2. Find your User ID displayed on the settings page
  3. Send an email to translateplayapp@gmail.com with:
    • Subject: "Delete My Account"
    • Your User ID

We will process your deletion request within 30 days and confirm via email once completed.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page, and the "Last updated" date will reflect the most recent changes.

11. Contact Us
